Literacy Overview

Academic literacy reflects my ability to engage with academic and technical material in a meaningful, disciplined, and structured way. Through Analytical Chemistry, I have learned how to read scientific texts, laboratory manuals, and academic resources with understanding and precision.

These experiences help me identify important concepts, follow complex instructions, and communicate clearly through well-organised academic writing.

Core Strengths

  • Structured academic writing through reports, assignments, and analytical tasks
  • Clear explanation of methods, results, and conclusions
  • Critical thinking and evidence-based reasoning
  • Improved attention to detail and professional communication
  • Growing confidence in reading, interpreting, and presenting information
