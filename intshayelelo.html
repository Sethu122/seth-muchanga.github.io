Module Introduction

Intshayelelo 101 is a first-year support module designed to help students transition smoothly from secondary school to university life. It introduces important academic, ethical, digital, and social ideas that help students settle into higher education.

This e-portfolio reflects how I understand the module and how the learning areas contribute to my growth as a student.
